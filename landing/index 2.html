FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Docodile compliant with Indian healthcare practices? +

Yes — Docodile is built around Indian OPD workflows: digital prescriptions, GST-aware billing, ABDM-ready records and bilingual patient cards.

How is patient data protected? +

Each clinic's data is logically isolated. We use JWT-based auth, role-based access, encrypted database connections and full audit trails.

Can we migrate from our current system? +

Yes. We help import patient lists, history and inventory from spreadsheets or your existing software during onboarding.

Does it work in low-bandwidth areas? +

Docodile is optimised for poor networks and gracefully handles intermittent connectivity, queueing local changes when needed.
